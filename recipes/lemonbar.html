Lemon Bars Recipe

How to make lemon bars

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Powedered sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Granulated sugar
  • Lemon juice
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350F and spray a 13x9-inch pan with non-stick spray.
  2. Add the flour, powdered sugar, and butter to a mixing bowl. Use your hands to mix the ingredients until it forms a crumbly dough.
  3. Put the dough into the prepared pan and press it until itâ€™s smooth and even.
  4. Put the pan in the oven and bake until the crust is golden brown on the edges. This should take 20-25 minutes.
  5. While the crust is baking, add the sugar, eggs, and lemon juice to a stand mixer. Whisk on medium speed until the sugar has dissolved, and the eggs are light and fluffy. This should take about 4-5 minutes.
  6. Add the flour and baking powder to the lemon mixture, and mix until everything is well combined. Pour the lemon filling over the baked crust.
  7. Return the pan to the oven and bake for 22-25 minutes.
  8. Remove the lemon bars from the oven, and let them cool. Before serving, dust them with powdered sugar.
